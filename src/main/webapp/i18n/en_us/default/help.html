Egyptian Fortunes

Return to the golden age of ancient Egypt where the mysteries of the pyramids can divine rewards in Egyptian Fortunes! Find 4 or more Cartouches and win a prize. These mystery pyramids may also reveal Sceptre or Eye sub-symbols. Collect 4 Sceptre sub-symbols to trigger the Glyph Bonus, and win additional prizes or collect more Eye sub-symbols. Collect 4 Eye sub-symbols to trigger the Talisman Bonus, where you may reveal a multiplier up to 10x!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the minus sign (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the plus sign (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Egyptian Fortunes is to find 4 or more Cartouches and win a prize.

Press each glowing pyramid to reveal a Cartouche or a Scarab. Each revealed symbol may have a Sceptre sub-symbol or an Eye sub-symbol.

Reveal 4 or more Cartouches to win a prize based on the prize table. Revealing more Cartouches increases the prize amount.

Collect 4 Sceptre sub-symbols to trigger the Glyph Bonus. Select 4 of 8 glyph stones to reveal a symbol. If the revealed symbol matches one of the symbols in the circle, a prize or an Eye sub-symbol is awarded.

Collect 4 Eye sub-symbols to trigger the Talisman Bonus. Select a talisman to reveal a multiplier, which is applied to the total win.

Play ends when all symbols are revealed and any bonus games awarded are completed.

Dismiss the win message and view the game’s outcome by pressing the message.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Wins are shown in currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
